My Philosophy

I believe technology is at its best when it serves as a bridge to understanding. My goal is not just to build tools, but to create experiences that ignite curiosity and empower learners to explore complex concepts intuitively.

By blending the logical power of code with the visual clarity of 3D modeling and interactive graphing, we can make abstract ideas tangible. True learning happens when students can play, experiment, and build their own mental models in a hands-on digital environment.
